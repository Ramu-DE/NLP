# Understand how ***probabilities guide language models*** to form sentence

Language models assign **probabilties** to the next word for given context and use these probabities to choose a likely next world.

In order to generate entire sentences or texts ,the model repeatedly chooses a word according to the **probability distribution** over the next word and then adds the choosen word to the context.the model then uses this context to predict the next word until a full sentence or text has been generated.
